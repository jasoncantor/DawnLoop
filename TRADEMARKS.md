`DawnLoop`, the DawnLoop app name, icon, screenshots, bundle identifiers, and
related brand assets are not licensed for third-party App Store or marketplace
distribution.

You may refer to DawnLoop nominatively when discussing the project or linking to
the original repository, but you may not:

- publish your own build as `DawnLoop`;
- reuse the DawnLoop icon or product screenshots for your fork;
- use the same bundle identifier in a redistributed build; or
- imply endorsement, partnership, or official status.

If you fork the project and share it outside your household or organization,
rename it and replace the visual branding.
