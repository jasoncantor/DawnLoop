# DawnLoop Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 12, 2026

## Overview

DawnLoop is an iPhone app that helps you create and manage light-based wake-up automations with Apple Home. DawnLoop does not require an account, does not operate a backend service, and does not sell your data.

## Data DawnLoop Accesses

DawnLoop may access the following data on your device:

- HomeKit data, including your homes, selected accessories, scenes, and automation objects
- Optional location data, only if you grant When In Use access, to preview sunrise- and sunset-based alarm timing on your device
- Alarm configuration data that you create in the app, stored locally on your device

## How DawnLoop Uses Data

DawnLoop uses this data only to:

- show your available Apple Home homes and compatible lights
- let you create, edit, repair, enable, disable, and remove Home automations
- save your alarm settings locally on your device
- preview sunrise and sunset timing when location access is granted

## Data Sharing

DawnLoop does not send your personal data to a DawnLoop-operated server because DawnLoop does not use a backend service for app operation.

DawnLoop may rely on Apple frameworks such as HomeKit and system services to perform app features on your device. Any handling of data by Apple is governed by Apple's own terms and privacy policies.

## Tracking

DawnLoop does not use third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or cross-app tracking.

## Accounts

DawnLoop does not require account creation or sign-in.

## Data Retention

Alarm data you create in DawnLoop is stored locally on your device until you edit or delete it, or remove the app from your device.

## Your Choices

You can:

- deny or revoke Home access in iOS Settings
- deny or revoke location access in iOS Settings
- delete alarms you created in DawnLoop
- remove the app from your device to delete its local app data

## Children's Privacy

DawnLoop is not directed to children and does not knowingly collect personal information from children through a backend service.

## Changes to This Policy

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time. Material updates will be reflected by updating the date at the top of this page.
